Breakout

The thing I created was the game “Breakout”. The main reason why I chose to make this game is because I grew up playing it. It’s important to me because it reminds me of my childhood and it brings back good memories from the past. I made the game by using Piskel. Piskel is basically for anyone and/or everyone that wants to make something in pixels. I think this is one of the funnest/interesting projects I‘ve made so far because I got to chose what it was that i wanted to make rather than just doing something not so meaningful to me. But at the end I didn’t just have fun making this but I learned something new. Even though at the beginning I wanted to just quit because it wasn’t coming out the way I wanted it to but then I tried to slow down and it finally started to look like a real game.
