Sound Power Project:

"Because Education Is Knowledge"

In "Because Education is Knowledge" we created a 3d wood sculpture. We created an interpretation of soundwaves based from an audio recording of our voices saying the statement “Because education is knowledge”. We chose this statement because the words are powerful. It shows a message that is simple to understand but at the same time it's not so easy to know what we are trying to say. Our statement is powerful because it has two important words “education” and “knowledge” that are used all around the world and they are important to most people in order to succeed. Color adds to the overall meaning of the work by going from lightest blue to darkest blue. Once it starts to darken up it means the statement starts to become more powerful. At the end of this project we learned how to make a powerful statement and a few other things in the process.
