Final Product

For this project we got the chance to research and plan out a budget.I chose to be an Industrial Production Manager Career and I had to find a house,job, and a car.In the end this project was probably one of those types of projects that you could benefit from. For example, If I have trouble in the future on keeping track of how I spend my money I could come back to this spreadsheet and change a few numbers around and I will be able to see how I spend my money.Also I will be able to see what unnecessary things I spend my money on and stop spending money on them.
