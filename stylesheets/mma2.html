The Heart

The movie takes place in Portland,Oregon. Where a married couple live together. Edward and Diana; not as happy as they were before they still put up with each other.One day Edward meets a lady named Ashley. Ashley a 33 year old (divorced) they get close after a while. Diana gets a little crazy and jealous. Diana soon meets Ashley’s ex husband George and they start talking and getting closer as well. Diana falls for George but he doesn’t fall for Diana. Diana later ends up having mixed feelings for Edward and George. Edward doesn’t want to get divorced although he is cheating on Diana. He later leaves Ashley so he can make his marriage work.
